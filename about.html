About My Diary

Welcome to My Digital Diary – your personal space to record memories, plan your days, and track your finances.

✨ Features:

  • 📅 Calendar – Organize your days and never miss important events.
  • 📝 Diary Entries – Record your daily thoughts, reflections, and goals.
  • 💰 Expenditure Tracker – Keep track of your spending habits.

This diary is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It’s designed to be simple, elegant, and useful for everyday life.
